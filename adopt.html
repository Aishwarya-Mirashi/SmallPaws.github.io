LOOKING TOWARDS ADOPTING A DOG OR KITTEN? READ HERE ABOUT OUR RESCUES!

        
    Adopting an animal and bringing home a new family member is a truly wonderful feeling. At Small Paws hundreds of 
    
    animals wait in anticipation of loving homes. Many have been rescued from suffering on the streets from being 
    
    injured or just old. All are deserving of a forever home and the process is a lot easier than you think.

      Adopting an animal, dog or cat, is not something to be undertaken lightly. Cats can live as long as 15
    
    years and dogs upto 20 years. We want to ensure that any animal it places for adoption go to people who will 
    
    provide them with a clean, safe, family environment, will feed them properly, and will – above all – give 
    
    them love until the end of their days.
           
    Here are a few steps to adopt a pet from Small Paws:
            
            1.Be a 100% sure you want to adopt

            Read up. Talk to people. Be certain. In this case, it is a live animal, who eats, sleeps, breathes and FEELS. Take your time and be sure you are ready to adopt!

            2.Fill up the Dog or Cat Adoption form
            
            Take your time to fill this one. Be honest with your replies. We’re seasoned adoption filtering agents,we can sniff and detect discrepancies like hounds :)
